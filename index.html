About Me

I am a passionate recent interior design graduate based in Toronto. My fascination with design began early as I crafted dioramas and drew inspiration from the worlds of art and fashion. During high school, I landed an internship at an interior design company, pushing me to pursue Interior design as a career. My work always aims for sustainable design with lots of biophilia. Outside of design, I enjoy painting and long-distance running.

My Work

1 Strength St.

Project 1 strength street is a 2 bedroom, 2 bath basement apartment. The overall design is inspired by a "hotel chic" style as per the client's request. The final renderings were achieved in a combination of using Autocad, Sketchup, and Enscape.

Cabinetry

This project was in collaboration with a contractor. Given specific measurements and client style preferences, we curated the most suitable built-in wall cabinetry. This render was done using Google Sketchup and Enscape.

Age of Grace

As a Theis project, Age of Grace was constructed to be a center for domestic abuse victims. With over 20 rooms and multiple social spaces, Age of Grace takes on a biophilic, inclusive, and modern approach to its design process. Work created on Autocad, Sketchup, Enscape, and Sketch art tools.
